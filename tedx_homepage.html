About Us:

TEDxIITHyderabad is an initiative to bring the spirit of TED to the community of IIT Hyderabad. With a focus on "ideas worth spreading", TEDxIITH provides a platform for sharing innovative and thought-provoking ideas through captivating talks, performances, and interactive experiences. Driven by the belief in the power of ideas to drive positive change, TEDxIITH fosters an environment of intellectual curiosity, collaboration, and community-building.
